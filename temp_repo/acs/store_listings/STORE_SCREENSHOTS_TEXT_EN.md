## üí° PRO TIPS

### For App Store (iOS):
- Use portrait orientation (1242x2688px for iPhone 13 Pro Max)
- Maximum 10 screenshots
- First 3 screenshots are most important (visible without scrolling)
- Can use different screenshots for different languages

### For Google Play (Android):
- Use portrait orientation (1080x1920px)
- Maximum 8 screenshots
- First 2 screenshots visible in search results
- Can use video as first asset (recommended)

### Best Practices:
1. **Test headlines:** A/B test different headline variants
2. **Update seasonally:** Change Screen 1 for holiday shopping, etc.
3. **Feature rotation:** Swap Screen 6-7 to highlight new features
4. **Localize fully:** Don't just translate‚Äîadapt to market
5. **Monitor competitors:** Update if competitors change messaging
